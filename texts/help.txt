>>> Command-Line Text Translator <<<

Usage:
--help, -h
    Displays this menu.

--format <in> [out], -f <in> [out]
    Formats the latin text by appending macrons and stripping spaces.

--translate <in> [out], -t <in> [out]
    Translates and parses the latin text.

--formatted-translate <in> [out], -ft <in> [out]
    Formats the latin text and then translates and parses it.
